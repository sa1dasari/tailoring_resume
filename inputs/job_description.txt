Software Engineer II - Fulfillment
New York, New York, United States
StubHub is on a mission to redefine the live event experience on a global scale. Whether someone is looking to attend their first event or their hundredth, we’re here to delight them all the way from the moment they start looking for a ticket until they step through the gate. The same goes for our sellers. From fans selling a single ticket to the promoters of a worldwide stadium tour, we want StubHub to be the safest, most convenient way to offer a ticket to the millions of fans who browse our platform around the world.

The Fulfillment team ensures that every ticket delivered is valid. We own the post-purchase flow; including barcode integrations, mobile wallets, partner APIs, inventory reconciliation, observability, and incident response, with a mission of zero fulfillment breakage and a seamless experience for fans and sellers. 

As an Software Engineer II, you’ll build end-to-end post-purchase flows, partner integrations that directly drive reliability improvements and measurable business impact at massive scale. 

Location: Hybrid (3 days in office/2 days remote) – New York, NY or Century City, CA 
 
What You'll Do:
Design, implement, and operate backend services and integrations powering the post-purchase fulfillment pipeline (barcode validation, mobile wallets, partner APIs, & inventory reconciliation). 
Build 0-to-1 fault-tolerant systems and async pipelines (queues/streaming) that scale for peak event traffic and ensure idempotent, reliable delivery. 
Own features end-to-end: from requirements and architecture to implementation, testing, deployment, monitoring, and runbooks. 
Collaborate cross-functionally with Product, Data, and QA, as well as two Operations partners: 
Active Support: enhances the post-purchase customer experience and builds tools for fans and support agents. 
Marketplace Operations: drives inventory quality and supply across events. 
Instrument services with robust observability: metrics, logs, distributed traces, and alerting to proactively detect and reduce fulfillment breakage. 
Why You'll Love This Team:

Fast-Paced, Ambiguous Engineering Culture: Thrive in an environment that encourages bold experimentation, rapid iteration, and innovative problem-solving. 
Exposure to AI-Driven Experiences: Work on real-world products that integrate AI-powered functionality, shaping the way fans interact with our platform. 
Meaningful Product Impact: Build the technological backbone of our marketplace, delivering reliable, scalable systems that power millions of ticket transactions and directly reduce post-purchase failures. 
Collaborative Engineering Environment: Join a team of talented engineers who value thoughtful execution, shared ownership, and celebrating wins together. 
Continuous Feedback and Growth: Enjoy a culture centered on regular, constructive feedback to refine your technical skills, deepen your product judgment, and grow as an engineer. 
What You've Done:

3-4 years building production software with a focus on backend and distributed systems. 
Proficient in Java, Python, or Golang, with experience building and consuming APIs. 
Built scalable, reliable systems for real-time ticketing, barcode validation, and post-purchase workflows. 
Designed async pipelines and streaming systems (Kafka or similar) to handle peak traffic with zero breakage. 
Integrated third-party APIs, with bonuses for experience in venue systems, barcode providers, or mobile wallets. 
Operated cloud services (AWS, GCP, Azure), delivering features that improve reliability and power seamless experiences at scale.
